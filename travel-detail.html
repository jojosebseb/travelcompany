Komodo Islands is an archipelago of 7 thousand islands and islets situated North East from Sulawesi shores in the gulf of Tomini. It takes time and determination to go there, making it even more worthy for true adventure-seekers. Something’s worth having is never easy! Its most famous island is Kadidiri Island. The Komodo dragon became something of a living legend. In the decades since the Komodo was discovered, various scientific expeditions from a range of countries have carried out field research on the dragons on Komodo Island

Its exotic beauty and charms make it one of the key tourist destinations of the area. Kadidiri is a paradise for divers for its stunning underwater landscapes, rich coral reefs and exquisite marine life. Also meet Bajo Tribe who’s also often being called the Sea Gypsies, Bajo indigenous people spend most of their lives drifting ocean waves. Swim with stingless jellyfish, bonfire under million stars night sky, and anything of a Paradise you can dream of is here! Last but not least, the volcano island called Una Una Island has the most exotic underwater garden among all. The colors are like beyond your imagination! Let's explore this paradise together!
